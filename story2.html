🍌 The Talking Banana!

English:

One morning, Baby wanted to eat a banana. 🍌

But the banana said, “Hey! Don’t eat me yet!” 😲

Baby jumped back. “You can talk?” 😳

“Yes,” said the banana. “But I only talk when someone is very kind.” 💛

Mami smiled, “Baby is very kind.” 😊

The banana laughed and said, “Okay, now you can eat me, I’m ready!” 😋

Hausa:

Wata safiya, Baby yana so ya ci ayaba. 🍌

Amma ayaban ta ce, “Hey! Kada ka cinye ni yanzu!” 😲

Baby ya firgita, “Kai! Kina magana ne?” 😳

“Eh,” ayaban ta ce. “Amma fa bana magana sai idan mutum yana da kauna sosai.” 💛

Mami ta yi murmushi, “Baby yana da kauna sosai.” 😊

Ayaban ta yi dariya ta ce, “Toh yanzu zaka iya cin ni, na shirya!” 😋
