📖 Mami & Baby's Funny Story

English:

One day, Mami said: “Baby, don’t run!”

But Baby ran fast like a rocket 🚀

He jumped over toys, food, and even shoes!

Mami shouted, “I’ll catch you!”

Baby said, “Catch me if you can!”

They laughed until they slept on the floor 😴💖

Hausa:

Wata rana, Mami ta ce: “Baby, kar ka gudu!”

Amma Baby ya tashi kamar roka 🚀

Ya tsallake kayan wasa, abinci, har takalma!

Mami ta ce, “Zan kama ka!”

Baby ya ce, “Ki kamo ni idan kin iya!”

Suka yi dariya har suka yi barci a kasa 😴💙
